About Me

I was born in Shanghai, China, but moved to Melbourne, Australia when I was 12.
I am currently an undergraduate student at New York University majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Maths and Cybersecurity.
I am interested in the field of robotics, and this led me to join NYU’s RoboSub VIP team that built an Autonomous Underwater Vehicle to participate in the RoboSub competition. I am the team lead of the Motion team in RoboSub and have worked on writing the code for controlling the thrusters.
I am also interested in the field of machine learning and have participated in the research of adversarial machine learning. Working as a student researcher in NYUAD's Center for Cybersecurity, I worked with other researchers to write a program that could generate a patch to hide people from object detectors.
In my spare time, I enjoy reading, especially novels and history books, as well as playing with my dog, Cookie.
